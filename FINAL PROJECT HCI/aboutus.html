About Us

History

The Jockey's Conference Center (JCC) at Gelora Bung Karno Stadium holds a prestigious history as a versatile venue for national and international events. With top-notch facilities, it has successfully hosted important conferences, exhibitions, fairs, indoor sports competitions, and musical concerts. Situated at the heart of Gelora Bung Karno Stadium, the JCC remains a symbol of Indonesia's commitment to hosting remarkable events that bring together diverse industries and cultural experiences.
